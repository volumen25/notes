Emptiness

2025-12-25

Clay transforms into pottery through craft, but it’s the emptiness within that makes a pot truly useful. What’s absent can be just as significant as what’s present. This idea originates from the Tao Te Ching, a foundational text of Taoism. (Tzu & Needleman, 1989)

Reference

Tzu, L., & Needleman, J. (1989). Tao Te Ching: Text Only Edition (G.-F. Feng, J. English, & T. Lippe, Trans.; Reprint edition). Vintage.
